  • Mac: command + shift + z

Application Tips

  • Keep your responses concise. Recruiters often scan through applications quickly.
  • Use consistent formatting. Ensure that dates, job titles, and other details are formatted consistently throughout your application.
  • Tailor your application. Ensure that your responses align with the specific role or company you're applying for.
  • Save regularly. Don't risk losing your data by relying solely on the tool. Regularly save or backup your responses elsewhere.
  • Review before submission. Always double-check your application for errors or omissions.
